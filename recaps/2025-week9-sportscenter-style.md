### **The Photo Finish: Bruce's Team (112.5) vs Sean's Team (112.0)**

**STOP THE PRESSES! **Bruce rode the tight end revolution to victory, with Brock Bowers putting up a position-leading 33 fantasy points. The defense chipped in with a massive 17.5-point bonus, the second-highest of the week, proving that sometimes the best offense really is a good defense.**

**But let's talk about Sean's heartbreak for a moment. Joe Flacco - JOE FLACCO! - turned back the clock a decade and threw for 37 fantasy points, the highest individual score of the week. Justin Herbert backed him up with 30 points of his own. That's 67 points from your quarterbacks! In any other matchup, against any other team, Sean's walking away victorious. But fantasy football is a cruel mistress, and despite Garrett Wilson and Rhamondre Stevenson being sidelined, Sean came within a single dropped pass, a single yard, a single ANYTHING of victory.**

**At 6-3, Bruce keeps pace in the playoff race. Sean falls to 3-6, and this one's going to sting. This is the loss that keeps you up at night, the one you'll remember come Week 14 if you miss the playoffs by one game. Half a point. In fantasy football, that's the difference between ecstasy and agony.**

**The result? Aaron improves to 2-7, which isn't good, but it's better than 1-8! Mitch falls to 3-6, and with that injury list looking like a medical journal, the road ahead appears treacherous. This is where seasons go to die, folks - not with a bang, but with a whimper.**
