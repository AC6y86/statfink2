### Prologue: Murder at Highbury Manor

The grandfather clock in the foyer struck midnight as the last of the regular season guests departed Highbury Manor. Twelve fantasy managers had gathered here sixteen weeks ago, each harboring dreams of championship glory. Now, after months of calculated roster moves and agonizing start/sit decisions, only four remained for the playoff finals.

Inspector Hardcastle lit his pipe and surveyed the drawing room. Four suspects stood before him: Matt, Chris, Bruce, and Dan. By morning, the championship would be decided. By morning, dreams would die.

"This week," the Inspector announced, "there can be no appeals, no second chances. Head-to-head combat. Winner takes all."

The suspects exchanged nervous glances. Someone in this room would commit the perfect fantasy crime. Someone else would be the victim.

Bruce examined his bronze medal with quiet satisfaction. Chase Brown's three-touchdown explosion had delivered him to the podium.

Dan poured himself a whiskey and contemplated what might have been. Seven zeros. Seven ghosts who haunted his championship dreams.

The case is closed. The champion crowned. The mystery solved.
